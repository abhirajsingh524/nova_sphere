#NOVA_SPHERE

Nova Sphere AI is an AI-driven disaster management platform focused on public safety and preparedness.
India faces 1000+ disasters yearly, with most people lacking proper training.
The project solves gaps like no unified system, poor rural access, and lack of multilingual support.
It offers a single super app for education, alerts, and coordination.
Core features include AI chatbot, real-time alerts, and multilingual voice/text support.
It also provides offline IVR access for low-connectivity areas.
Includes training modules and AR-based disaster simulations.
Built with scalable tech (AI, cloud, APIs) for nationwide deployment.
Targets citizens, government, responders, and healthcare sectors.
Aims to reduce casualties and economic losses through early preparedness.
